# Getting Started

This section covers how to access the system for the first time, log in to your account, and reset your password if needed.

## Registration via Invitation

New users cannot self-register. An Admin or Head Contractor must invite you to the system.

### How It Works

1. You receive an email invitation with a registration link
2. Click the link — it takes you to the registration page with your email pre-filled
3. Complete the registration form:
   - **Full name** — Your display name in the system
   - **Password** — Must meet the password requirements (minimum 8 characters, at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character)
4. Click **Register** to create your account
5. You are automatically logged in and taken to the dashboard

### What to Expect

- Your role is pre-assigned by the person who invited you (Subcontractor, Head Contractor, Client, or Admin)
- You cannot change your own role — only an Admin can do this
- Your invitation link expires after 7 days. If it expires, ask the person who invited you to resend it

## Logging In

### Steps

1. Navigate to the application URL
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Login**
4. You are taken to the project dashboard

### Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Invalid credentials" error | Double-check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive |
| Account deactivated | Contact your Admin to reactivate your account |
| Forgot password | Use the password reset flow below |

## Password Reset

If you forget your password, you can reset it without contacting an Admin.

### Steps

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**
2. Enter the email address associated with your account
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam/junk folders if not found)
5. Click the link in the email — it takes you to the reset password page
6. Enter your new password (must meet the same requirements as registration)
7. Click **Reset Password**
8. You are redirected to the login page — log in with your new password

### Important Notes

- The reset link expires after **1 hour**
- Only one active reset link exists at a time — requesting a new one invalidates the previous link
- If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or try again

## Role-Specific Notes

| Role | Registration Notes |
|------|-------------------|
| **Subcontractor** | Typically invited by a Head Contractor for a specific project |
| **Head Contractor** | Invited by an Admin during project setup |
| **Client** | Invited by an Admin or Head Contractor for oversight access |
| **Admin** | Created during initial system setup or invited by another Admin |

## Tips & Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: No. Your email is your unique identifier. Contact an Admin if you need to use a different email.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**  
A: No. Each email address can only be associated with one account.

**Q: What happens if my account is deactivated?**  
A: You cannot log in or access any resources. Your data remains in the system. An Admin can reactivate your account at any time.

**Q: I never received my invitation email. What should I do?**  
A: Check your spam/junk folder. If still not found, ask the person who invited you to resend the invitation from the User Administration panel.
